What You’ll Learn

  • Basic Skills: balancing, starting, stopping, and safe falling techniques.
  • Intermediate Techniques: smooth gliding, controlled turns, posture and stride.
  • Advanced Moves: speed basics, simple jumps, and performance routines.
  • Safety & Gear: helmet fitting, wrist/elbow/knee protection, and warm-up routines.
